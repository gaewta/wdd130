Rio Rafting Co

Rio Rafting has done everything we can to make your experience safe and exciting. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a seasoned veteran, we can make your experience a great one. We have invested in the latest and greatest watercraft to ensure you get the experience you deserve. And our trained guides have years of rafting experience and are hilarious. There is a variety of adventures available for all ages to enjoy. Some parts of the river are gentle, while others are high adventure parts. Whether you are looking for a nature fix, a new hobby, or a thrill-seeker, we welcome you!

History

Rafting has a short history in Japan and there are still many people who are unaware of this amazing adventure. Rio Rafting is a start-up company established to revitalize rural areas and promote new outdoor activities by taking advantage of Japan's abundant nature. Together with our sponsors who agree with our management policy, we will spread the charm of White Water Rafting and bring big smiles to everyone's faces!
